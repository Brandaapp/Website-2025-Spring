Branda Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2025

Summary

Branda is committed to protecting your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or process any personal information. It is designed solely to display information and provide helpful links. No user accounts or sign-ins are required, and all data interactions occur externally through links to official Brandeis University websites.

Facilities Work Orders

For facilities work orders, Branda provides a webview link to the official Brandeis facilities request page. Branda does not collect or store any data related to these requests. You can access the facilities request page here.

Analytics

Branda does not track or collect any usage analytics.

Modifications to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Updates will be communicated through the app, and continued use of the app constitutes agreement to the updated policy.
