Branda

What it is

Branda is a mobile app designed to provide students of Brandeis with quick and easy access to essential campus information and services. These include News, Campus Hours (Dining, Gosman, and Library), Campus Map and links to facilities work orders through official Brandeis websites. Branda is developed and maintained by Brandeis alumni and The Branda Team, which consists of over twenty current undergraduate students.
